About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineering professional with 2 years of experience in project planning, performance reporting, and process optimisation — now deepening my expertise through Industrial Engineering and International Management (M.Sc.) at Hochschule Fresenius in Cologne.

Before returning to academia, I worked as a Project Planner at Godrej & Boyce Manufacturing, one of India's largest corporations, where I built Power BI dashboards, reduced supply chain reporting lead time by 30%, and led quality audits that cut recurring defects by 25% across different sites.

I hold a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt certification and bring a practical, data-driven mindset to every problem I work on.
