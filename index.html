Project Case Studies

Detailed breakdown of key projects with methodology and measurable results.

Supply Chain Reporting Transformation

Godrej & Boyce · 2023–2024

Power BI SAP ERP Data Analytics

Challenge

Manual reporting from multiple SAP sources. Senior management waited days for outdated data. No single view of procurement KPIs.

Approach

Built 5+ Power BI dashboards with star schema data model. Connected procurement tables, project milestones, and vendor metrics. Automated SAP data extraction.

Results

~30%

Reduction in reporting lead time. Real-time KPIs replaced weekly email reports. Faster, data-driven decision making across leadership.

Quality Audit & Defect Reduction

Godrej & Boyce · 2023–2024

DMAIC Six Sigma Quality

Challenge

Recurring defects across multiple sites despite corrective actions. Same issues reappearing quarter after quarter. No root-cause standardisation.

Approach

Applied full DMAIC cycle. Pareto analysis identified 3 categories = 70% of defects. Root cause: inconsistent documentation. Standardised procedures and visual work instructions.

Results

25%

Reduction in recurring defects. Standardised audit checklists across all sites. Trend dashboards for immediate deviation visibility.

3D Simulation with Augmented Reality

Hochschule Fresenius · 2025–2026

AR AI Industry 4.0

Challenge

Gap between CAD models on screens and physical shop floors. No spatial context for layout decisions. Slow onboarding of new operators.

Approach

Developed AI-driven visualisation tools integrating 3D CAD with AR headsets. ML models analysed production data to predict bottlenecks and suggest optimal layouts.

Results

Research

Completed prototype demonstrating spatial process visualisation. Applications in training, layout planning, maintenance guidance, and quality inspection.
